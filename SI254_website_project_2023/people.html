The Money Makers

There are many faces on WallStreet but these 3 stand out the most

Warren Buffett

Warren Buffett, born on August 30, 1930, in Omaha, Nebraska, is widely regarded as one of the most successful investors of all time. He is the Chairman and CEO of Berkshire Hathaway, a multinational conglomerate holding company. Buffett's investment philosophy emphasizes value investing and long-term holding, often acquiring companies with strong fundamentals and competitive advantages. He is known for his humility, frugality, and down-to-earth communication style. Often referred to as the "Oracle of Omaha," Buffett shares his investment insights and wisdom through annual shareholder letters and interviews, garnering a massive following and respect within the investment community and beyond.

Larry Fink

Laurence "Larry" Fink, born on November 2, 1952, is the Chairman and CEO of BlackRock, the world's largest asset management firm. Fink co-founded BlackRock in 1988 and has since built it into a global powerhouse managing trillions of dollars in assets. He is known for his influence on global financial markets and often shares insights on economic and investment trends. Fink is recognized for advocating responsible corporate governance and sustainability, urging companies to consider their societal impact and long-term sustainability alongside financial performance. Under his leadership, BlackRock has emphasized environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors in investment decision-making.

Jordan Belfort

Jordan Belfort, born on July 9, 1962, in New York, is a former stockbroker and motivational speaker. He is best known for founding the brokerage firm Stratton Oakmont, infamous for its involvement in pump-and-dump schemes and securities fraud during the 1990s. Belfort's story is depicted in the movie "The Wolf of Wall Street," based on his memoir of the same name. Following legal troubles and serving time in prison, Belfort transformed his life, becoming a motivational speaker and author, focusing on ethics in business and sales strategies.
